# 02_英文剧本

## Quick Reference

- Project Title: ice cold
- Logline: When a donor family's star freshman and the brother of the boy he once helped destroy fake a relationship to contain a campus scandal, they uncover an abuse archive that can either save a sister or burn the whole hockey empire down.
- Target Market: NA-English
- Genre: Queer Campus Scandal Romance / Enemies to Lovers / Fake Dating
- Episode Count: 24
- Episode Duration: 1-2 min
- Core Mood / Visual Tags: bruise-blue winter, cold prestige, public scandal, dangerous tenderness
- Protagonist Snapshot: Ezra speaks like a blade. Cade survives like a bruise that learned how to hit back.

## Character Snapshot

- CHAR-01 Ezra Quinn: truth engine
- CHAR-02 Cade Mercer: pressure vessel and romantic detonator
- CHAR-03 Pastor Daniel Mercer: patriarchal antagonist
- CHAR-04 June Mercer: danger courier
- CHAR-05 Milo Hart: team ally
- CHAR-06 Tessa Vale: strategist friend
- CHAR-07 Noah Quinn: returning witness
- CHAR-08 Coach Brennan: institutional enforcer
- CHAR-09 Miriam Holt: system shield
- CHAR-10 Brock Danner: locker-room bully
