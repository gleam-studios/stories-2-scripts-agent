# 01_英文大纲

## Quick Reference

- Project Title: ice cold
- Logline: When a donor family's star freshman and the brother of the boy he once helped destroy fake a relationship to contain a campus scandal, they uncover an abuse archive that can either save a sister or burn the whole hockey empire down.
- Target Market: NA-English
- Genre: Queer Campus Scandal Romance / Enemies to Lovers / Fake Dating
- Episode Count: 24
- Episode Duration: 1-2 min
- Core Mood / Visual Tags: bruise-blue winter, cold prestige, public scandal, dangerous tenderness
- Protagonist Snapshot: Ezra weaponizes truth. Cade weaponizes endurance until endurance stops being enough.

## Adaptation Positioning

- Core Hook: A fake campus romance becomes the cover story for stealing an abuse archive out from under a donor-family hockey machine.
- Audience Positioning: NA-English short-drama viewers who respond to public reversals, romantic tension, damaged power boys, and institutional takedowns.
- Similarity Avoidance Direction: Rename the world, collapse geography into one elite campus, replace routine slow-burn progression with a scandal-and-evidence engine, and move the climax into public media, hearing, and arena spaces.

## Season Outline

- Story Premise: Ezra Quinn and Cade Mercer fake a relationship to survive a viral campus rumor, only to discover June Mercer has already started smuggling proof of an athletics-and-church abuse cover-up.
- Main Conflict: Cade must choose between the silence that has kept June alive and the truth that could free both of them, while Ezra must decide whether exposing the system matters more than protecting the boy inside it.
- Arc Segments:
  - Launch: collision, rumor, fake couple, first archive clues
  - Rise: chemistry, stolen evidence, and family-system escalation
  - Storm: hearings, betrayal under coercion, and public ruin
  - Payoff: rescue, proof, collapse, and chosen love
- Major Twists:
  - June has been moving evidence long before Ezra got involved
  - Noah returns not to forgive Cade, but to test whether he deserves trust
  - Cade's apparent betrayal at the hearing is actually the price of keeping June alive
  - The final proof is not on the flash drive everyone is chasing, but on Noah's old helmet-cam card
- Final Payoff: the school loses narrative control, Daniel Mercer loses both image and custody power, and Ezra answers Cade's love in public after the truth lands.

## Phase Push

- EP01-EP05: Build the fake-dating engine and make the archive feel more valuable than the romance.
- EP06-EP11: Let desire and danger rise together. Public hand-holds get riskier. Evidence gets closer.
- EP12-EP16: Break trust under hearings, leaks, and family coercion.
- EP17-EP20: Force active choice. Cade and Ezra stop reacting and start setting traps.
- EP21-EP24: Deliver rescue, proof, institutional fall, and emotional thaw.

## Episode Outline

### EP01 - The Collision

- Arc Phase: launch
- Logline: A donor-backed midnight run throws Ezra and Cade into the same frame, while June drops a flash drive no one is supposed to see.
- Core Event: Ezra realizes the golden boy in front of him is tied to Noah's past and to June's panic.
- Turn: The flash drive lands in Ezra's pocket instead of Cade's hand.
- End Hook: Ezra opens the drive and finds a folder labeled `DISCIPLINE ARCHIVE`.

### EP02 - Keep Your Story Straight

- Arc Phase: launch
- Logline: June begs Ezra to hide the drive, and Cade tries to keep the fallout from becoming a board-level scandal.
- Core Event: A viral clip turns one collision into campus rumor.
- Turn: Athletics summons both boys to a conduct hearing.
- End Hook: Ezra realizes he can make Cade need him.

### EP03 - Say We Lied

- Arc Phase: launch
- Logline: Ezra offers Cade a deal: fake-date him in public, and Ezra buries the drive until Cade helps expose the old assault cover-up.
- Core Event: The two boys turn leverage into a performance.
- Turn: Cade chooses the fake relationship over immediate destruction.
- End Hook: He confirms the rumor on camera.

### EP04 - Public Enough

- Arc Phase: launch
- Logline: The first fake date at Crowbar reads more convincing than either of them expected.
- Core Event: Brock weaponizes the rumor inside the rink.
- Turn: June vanishes after warning Ezra the real rot is still inside athletics.
- End Hook: Ezra finds her hiding above the chapel.

### EP05 - The Wrong Family

- Arc Phase: launch
- Logline: Ezra and Cade extract June from the chapel loft and nearly lose the archive in the process.
- Core Event: June reveals there is more than one copy of the proof.
- Turn: Pastor Daniel Mercer sees Ezra clearly for the first time.
- End Hook: Daniel says, "Quinn. You're Noah Quinn's brother."

### EP06 - Audio Proof

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: Ezra tells Cade exactly why he hates him, then plays the first surviving audio file from the buried assault.
- Core Event: Old violence becomes present tense.
- Turn: Cade hears Coach Brennan ordering silence after Noah's attack.
- End Hook: Brennan tells Cade his future depends on forgetting what he heard.

### EP07 - Practice Blood

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: Brock targets Cade at practice while Ezra receives the first direct threat to back off.
- Core Event: Milo notices Cade is being isolated on purpose.
- Turn: Ezra is warned Noah's sealed file will be dumped if he keeps digging.
- End Hook: Ezra decides to dig harder.

### EP08 - Donor Lights

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: At a donor gala, Cade is ordered to deny the rumor and publicly reassert family purity.
- Core Event: Ezra and Cade perform couple chemistry under real pressure.
- Turn: Cade refuses the script.
- End Hook: He takes Ezra's hand in front of donors, cameras, and his father.

### EP09 - Almost

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: The public hand-hold changes the campus temperature and the private one nearly becomes a kiss.
- Core Event: Ezra tends Cade's split knuckles on the footbridge at dawn.
- Turn: Both realize the fake part is decaying fast.
- End Hook: June's camera captures the almost-kiss.

### EP10 - Off The Record

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: Ezra and Cade stop pretending in the podcast booth and kiss for real.
- Core Event: Emotional control breaks in Ezra's strongest room.
- Turn: The person walking back onto campus is Noah Quinn.
- End Hook: Noah sees Cade before Cade is ready to be seen.

### EP11 - The Witness Returns

- Arc Phase: rise
- Logline: Noah warns Ezra that wanting Cade is not the same as trusting him.
- Core Event: Noah agrees to review the archive.
- Turn: The drive goes missing before that can happen.
- End Hook: Someone inside Ezra's circle is already being watched.

### EP12 - Conduct Review

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: A leaked private kiss gives the school the excuse it wanted to suspend Cade.
- Core Event: Holt reframes the scandal as a professionalism issue, not a violence issue.
- Turn: Tessa traces the leak toward Mercer-controlled territory.
- End Hook: Ezra realizes Daniel is no longer only policing home.

### EP13 - Backup Copy

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: June escapes again, bruised, carrying news that her father found one archive copy but not all of them.
- Core Event: Cade finally sees the damage on June's body, not just on his own.
- Turn: Ezra moves both Mercers toward the inn safehouse.
- End Hook: Cade admits Daniel had a conversion-camp plan ready for him.

### EP14 - Safehouse Rules

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: In the inn, privacy makes honesty harder to avoid.
- Core Event: Cade admits he learned obedience by making himself smaller than his own want.
- Turn: Daniel files a kidnapping accusation against Ezra.
- End Hook: The safehouse is no longer truly safe.

### EP15 - Take The Deal

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: Coach Brennan offers Cade a clean future if he lets Ezra become the liar in the official record.
- Core Event: Ezra cuts the first public teaser from the audio files.
- Turn: Cade hides what Brennan offered instead of sharing it.
- End Hook: The hearing date arrives before either boy is ready.

### EP16 - Say Nothing

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: Cade appears to betray Ezra by refusing to support him in the hearing room.
- Core Event: Holt takes silence as proof of instability on Ezra's side.
- Turn: Ezra sees the text on Cade's phone: `They have my sister.`
- End Hook: Betrayal becomes coercion in a single glance.

### EP17 - Trade Window

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: Ezra and Cade rebuild a working alliance to move June and the remaining files at the same time.
- Core Event: Brock intercepts the handoff near the rink.
- Turn: Cade takes responsibility for the resulting violence to get June clear.
- End Hook: The school now has a reason to bury him for good.

### EP18 - Live Mic

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: The boys set a trap in a donor lounge by wiring the room and forcing Daniel to speak plainly.
- Core Event: June becomes bait only because she chooses to.
- Turn: Daniel threatens her on a live microphone.
- End Hook: Ezra has the cleanest proof yet.

### EP19 - Too Early For Love

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: Holt suppresses the audio and proves the system is bigger than Daniel alone.
- Core Event: Ezra almost loses faith in public truth.
- Turn: Cade tells him, "I love you," on the footbridge.
- End Hook: Ezra cannot say it back.

### EP20 - Freeze

- Arc Phase: storm
- Logline: Ezra publishes without Cade's consent because silence now feels like complicity.
- Core Event: Cade feels used at the exact moment he needed trust.
- Turn: Daniel's people pull Cade back to the house before finals week.
- End Hook: June's burner phone goes dark.

### EP21 - Church Steps

- Arc Phase: payoff
- Logline: Ezra and Noah reach the Mercer property too late to stop the beating, but not too late to stop the lie.
- Core Event: Ezra finds Cade bloodied outside the church office.
- Turn: Cameras catch Ezra striking Daniel on the steps.
- End Hook: The truth is finally visible, but not yet winnable.

### EP22 - Altered Footage

- Arc Phase: payoff
- Logline: June is moved toward emergency safety while Holt releases edited footage that flips victim and aggressor.
- Core Event: Public sympathy starts tilting the wrong way again.
- Turn: Noah reveals he kept a hidden helmet-cam card from the original assault year.
- End Hook: The actual master proof still exists.

### EP23 - Black Tape

- Arc Phase: payoff
- Logline: Before the semifinal, Milo turns the team into a visible act of dissent.
- Core Event: Ezra feeds the helmet-cam file into the arena system.
- Turn: The scoreboard starts playing the buried attack during live competition.
- End Hook: The entire building sees what the school protected.

### EP24 - Thaw

- Arc Phase: payoff
- Logline: The school loses control of the narrative, Daniel loses the right to define his children, and Ezra finally answers Cade without hiding.
- Core Event: Holt and Brennan are removed, Daniel is taken out under public glare, and June chooses where home will be.
- Turn: Cade wins on the ice without surrendering himself to it.
- End Hook: Final payoff episode resolves the love line with a public kiss and a later quiet beat where Ezra dedicates the first page of his new project to Cade.
