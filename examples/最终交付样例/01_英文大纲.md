## Quick Reference

- Project Title: Midnight Heiress
- Logline: A dead heiress returns under a false name to reclaim her empire in public.
- Target Market: NA-English
- Genre: Hidden Identity / Revenge Romance
- Episode Count: 40
- Episode Duration: 1-2 min
- Core Mood / Visual Tags: glossy, cold luxury, public humiliation, dangerous attraction
- Protagonist Snapshot: Nora weaponizes composure, evidence, and public witness.

---

## Adaptation Positioning

- **Core Hook**: The presumed-dead heiress walks into her family's gala under another name and starts dismantling their power in front of cameras.
- **Audience Positioning**: Female-skewing overseas short-drama audience seeking status reversal, revenge payoff, and high-tension romance.
- **Similarity Avoidance Direction**: Shift conflict into public elite venues, boardroom legitimacy warfare, and fast alliance pressure — not household confrontations.

---

## Season Outline

- **Story Premise**: The real heiress returns under an alias to destroy the lie holding her cousin on the throne.
- **Main Conflict**: Nora must win legitimacy in public before the board can erase her again.

### Arc Segments

| Phase | Goal | Episode Span |
|-------|------|-------------|
| launch | False-name return, first public reversal, male lead enters the war | EP01–EP08 |
| rise | Alliance deepens, public wins stack, evidence becomes addictive | EP09–EP20 |
| storm | Betrayal, false defeat, villain pressure closes from all sides | EP21–EP32 |
| finale | Public truth revealed, board collapses, Nora reclaims power without surrender | EP33–EP40 |

### Major Twists

1. Adrian knows Eva is lying earlier than he admits — and chose to protect her anyway.
2. The forgery ring reaches the board, implicating a director Nora initially trusted.
3. Nora's legal identity was erased through a document her own father signed under duress.

### Final Payoff

Nora reclaims her name in public, with Adrian beside her by choice — not contract.

### EP01 — Back From the Grave

- **arc_phase**: launch
- **Logline**: Nora returns to the Hale charity gala as Eva Stone and survives Celia's first public strike.
- **Core Event**: Celia publicly humiliates Eva without knowing who she is.
- **Turn**: Adrian notices Eva knows family-only information no outsider should have.
- **Emotional Goal**: Audience feels the pleasure of watching a predator underestimate the wrong person.
- **Relationship Shift**: Nora and Adrian move from strangers to mutual threat — he is watching her now.
- **Antagonist Pressure**: Celia deploys social exclusion to remove Eva from the gala's power circle.
- **Satisfaction Beat**: `status_reversal` — Nora deflects Celia's public dismissal with a single line that leaves Celia with no clean move.
- **End Hook**: `identity_tease` — A camera catches the scar on Nora's wrist — the mark only the real heiress had.

---

### EP02 — The Auction Trap

- **arc_phase**: launch
- **Logline**: Nora weaponizes a charity auction to expose one layer of forged legitimacy.
- **Core Event**: The family seal and a forged ledger entry collide in public view.
- **Turn**: A longtime donor questions Celia's legitimacy out loud — the first crack in the wall.
- **Emotional Goal**: Audience feels that Nora is already three steps ahead of everyone in the room.
- **Relationship Shift**: Adrian moves from observer to cautious participant — he asks Nora a direct question.
- **Antagonist Pressure**: Celia deploys a loyalist director to undercut Eva's credibility with the donors.
- **Satisfaction Beat**: `face_slap` — The forged seal is flagged publicly, forcing Celia to apologize to the room while Eva watches.
- **End Hook**: `evidence_drop` — The board is forced to ask who Eva really is — and someone in the room already knows.
