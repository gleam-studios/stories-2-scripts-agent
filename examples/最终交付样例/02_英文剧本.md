# STERLING BOND — English Screenplay
**Project:** Sterling Bond
**Logline:** A disgraced ex-CIA operative running a London pawnshop discovers the antique she just bought is the key to a cartel's billion-dollar account — and the cartel already knows her face.
**Target Market:** EN/US Short-Drama Platform
**Genre:** Thriller / Action
**Episodes:** 20 | **Duration:** ~90s / episode

---

## EPISODE 01

[EPISODE BRIEF]
Episode Plot: Maya Sterling, running a failing Soho pawnshop, reluctantly buys a silver pocket watch from a desperate stranger. Within an hour, a man in a tailored suit arrives and offers ten times the price with no explanation. Maya refuses; he leaves her a burner phone. That night, the stranger who sold it is found dead outside her back door.
Opening Hook: A cash register slammed shut on a man's fingers — Maya owns this room before we know her name.
Cold Open Focus: The silver pocket watch under the shop's UV lamp, a hairline engraving on the case revealing a string of numbers.
Closing Hook: The dead man's coat pocket holds a photograph — Maya's face, taken from outside her own window.
Core Satisfaction: Identity Overwhelm — Maya establishes dominance over a dangerous buyer with zero visible effort.
Target Emotion: Gripped and slightly afraid — viewer knows more than Maya does.

---

### SCENE 01.01
Time: Day
Location: INT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — SOHO, LONDON
Characters: MAYA STERLING, PIOTR VENN
Core Event: Piotr sells Maya the pocket watch for £200 — far below what he asks — under visible panic
Scene Result: Maya has the watch; Piotr leaves without looking back, which is wrong
Outline Ref: core_event

[OPENING STATE]
Midday light cuts through the dusty front window. The shop is dense with old silver, clocks, and cased instruments — every shelf full. Maya stands behind the counter, UV lamp on, checking hallmarks on a tray of rings. The cash register is open. There are no other customers.

[MAIN BODY]

The door bell strikes. A man — PIOTR VENN, 40s, coat too big, sweat at his hairline — pushes inside and looks back through the glass before he steps all the way in.

Maya doesn't look up.

MAYA
We close in twenty minutes.

Piotr puts a velvet pouch on the counter. His hand stays on it.

PIOTR
Silver pocket watch. Eighteen-eighty-three. Swiss lever escapement. Full hunter case.

Maya sets down the rings and picks up the pouch. She opens it without being invited.

The watch is beautiful. Heavy. She turns it under the UV lamp. A hairline engraving on the case catches the light — not decorative. Alphanumeric. Eight characters.

MAYA
Where'd you get it?

PIOTR
Estate. My uncle's.

MAYA
(not looking up)
Your uncle had very precise taste in engraving.

Piotr's jaw tightens.

PIOTR
Eight hundred. It's worth more.

Maya sets the watch down in the velvet. She slides it back across the counter toward him.

MAYA
Two hundred.

PIOTR
Five.

MAYA
Two. Or you take it somewhere else.

A beat. Piotr looks at the door again. He takes the £200.

He leaves without a receipt. He doesn't look back through the window.

Maya watches the door settle. She picks the watch back up.

[SCENE ENDING — setup]
She turns it under the UV lamp a second time. The eight-character string is too regular to be decorative. She photographs it with her phone before putting it in the back-room safe. Something about the way Piotr looked at the door stays with her.

### SCENE 01.02
Time: Day
Location: INT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — SOHO, LONDON
Characters: MAYA STERLING, ELLIOT MARSH
Core Event: A man in a suit offers £2,000 for the watch — no haggling, no explanation
Scene Result: Maya refuses; Elliot leaves her a burner phone and the number to call
Outline Ref: turn

[OPENING STATE]
Forty minutes later. Maya is at the back counter, laptop open, running the engraving through a format-match search. The watch sits beside the keyboard, still in its velvet pouch. The shop is empty. The front door opens.

[MAIN BODY]

ELLIOT MARSH, 50s, grey suit, no tie, enters without looking at anything on the shelves. He walks directly to the counter. His shoes are expensive and quiet.

He places a business card face-down on the counter between them.

ELLIOT
The watch you bought this afternoon. I'd like to purchase it.

MAYA
Shop's technically closed.

ELLIOT
Two thousand pounds. Cash. Today.

Maya doesn't reach for the card.

MAYA
It's not for sale.

ELLIOT
I haven't told you what it is yet.

MAYA
You haven't told me your name either.

A beat. Elliot picks the card back up. He sets it face-up this time — blank except for a printed phone number.

ELLIOT
When you change your mind.

MAYA
I won't.

Elliot looks at the pouch beside the laptop. Then at Maya. He doesn't argue. He turns and walks to the door.

He pauses with his hand on the frame.

ELLIOT (CONT'D)
Lock up carefully tonight, Ms. Sterling.

He leaves. The door swings shut.

Maya looks at the card. He used her name. She never told him.

[SCENE ENDING — reversal]
She opens the laptop back toward her. The search result is still loading. She picks up the business card and turns it over. On the back, in small handwriting: *Don't call the police.*

### SCENE 01.03
Time: Night
Location: EXT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — BACK ALLEY
Characters: MAYA STERLING, PIOTR VENN (deceased)
Core Event: Maya finds Piotr dead in the alley — and a photograph of herself in his pocket
Scene Result: Maya understands she has been marked; the watch is already dangerous
Outline Ref: turn

[OPENING STATE]
10 PM. The alley behind the shop is narrow — bins, one working lamp at the far end. Maya is taking out the day's rubbish. She is not particularly alert. She has not called the number.

[MAIN BODY]

She lifts the lid off the recycling bin. A sound — wrong — from two metres to her left.

She turns.

Piotr is on the ground, back against the wall, legs out at a bad angle. His coat is open. He is not moving. His eyes are open.

Maya goes still. She doesn't call out. She reads the scene first.

No visible wound from here. No blood. His hand is palm-up on the pavement — empty.

She crouches beside him. Two fingers to his neck. Nothing.

She does not panic. She stands, puts one hand flat against the wall, and controls her breathing.

His coat pocket is half open. Something white. The edge of a photograph.

She pulls it out with two fingers.

It's her. Taken through the front window of the shop. Today — she's wearing the same cardigan. The UV lamp is on in the background.

Maya turns the photograph over. Nothing written on it.

She stands. She looks at the far end of the alley, then at the street end. Both empty.

[SCENE ENDING — cliffhanger]
She does not go back inside. She does not call 999. She stands in the alley, holding the photograph of herself, and finally takes the burner card out of her pocket.

## EPISODE 02

[EPISODE BRIEF]
Episode Plot: Maya calls the number; Elliot arrives in under six minutes. He tells her the watch contains routing credentials for a dormant cartel account — and that two other people who touched it are already dead. Maya refuses to hand it over and demands leverage first. Elliot brings her inside the operation instead.
Opening Hook: Elliot's car is already on the street when Maya calls — he was watching.
Cold Open Focus: Maya's thumb hovering over the call button on the burner card, the dead man's photograph pinned under her other hand.
Closing Hook: The safe is open. The watch is gone. Maya didn't move it.
Core Satisfaction: Underdog Rise — Maya forces a senior intelligence operator to negotiate with her instead of around her.
Target Emotion: Charged and curious — who is Maya, really?

---

### COLD OPEN
Time: Night
Location: INT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — BACK OFFICE
Characters: MAYA STERLING
Core Event: Maya retrieves a hidden ID document from behind a loose panel — she is not who she appears to be
Scene Result: Viewer understands Maya has a prior life she has not disclosed
Outline Ref: setup

[OPENING STATE]
Maya is back inside. The rear door is bolted. She does not turn on the overhead light — only the desk lamp, angled low. She crouches at the far wall, runs her fingers along the baseboard, and finds the loose panel.

[MAIN BODY]

Behind the panel: a slim water-proof case. She opens it.

Inside: a US passport under a different surname, a decommissioned agency access card with the photo matching her face, and a folded paper document — heavy cardstock, official seal, partially redacted.

She reads the redacted document for a few seconds. Her expression doesn't change.

She puts everything back. She replaces the panel exactly.

She stands. She picks up the burner card.

[SCENE ENDING / END COLD OPEN]
She dials.

### SCENE 02.01
Time: Night
Location: EXT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — SOHO STREET / INT. SHOP DOORWAY
Characters: MAYA STERLING, ELLIOT MARSH
Core Event: Elliot arrives within six minutes; Maya confronts him about being watched
Scene Result: Elliot confirms it — and tells her the watch contains cartel account credentials
Outline Ref: core_event

[OPENING STATE]
Maya stands in the shop doorway, not inside and not on the pavement. She has the shop lights off behind her. The street is nearly empty. She is watching from the shadow.

[MAIN BODY]

A dark saloon pulls to the kerb — no plates visible from here.

Elliot gets out alone. He walks toward her without urgency.

MAYA
Six minutes.

ELLIOT
Traffic was light.

MAYA
You were already outside.

He doesn't deny it.

ELLIOT
Ms. Sterling —

MAYA
Stop calling me that.

A beat.

ELLIOT
You found Venn.

MAYA
Someone left him for me to find. That's different.

Elliot stops two metres away. He reaches inside his jacket — slowly, deliberately — and produces a folded document. He holds it out.

ELLIOT
The watch contains routing credentials. Encrypted in the case engraving. They unlock a dormant account — approximately nine hundred million US dollars. The people who own that account want it back.

Maya doesn't take the document.

MAYA
How many people have touched the watch?

ELLIOT
Before you? Four.

MAYA
And?

ELLIOT
Venn makes three.

A long pause. Maya looks at the document in his outstretched hand.

MAYA
What happened to the fourth?

ELLIOT
She's standing in a doorway in Soho.

[SCENE ENDING — reversal]
Maya takes the document. She opens it. It is a photograph of herself — not from today. Different country, different clothes, different year. She is in the photograph with someone whose face has been cut out.

MAYA
Where did you get this?

ELLIOT
From the people who are coming for you.

### SCENE 02.02
Time: Night
Location: INT. STERLING PAWNSHOP — BACK OFFICE
Characters: MAYA STERLING, ELLIOT MARSH
Core Event: Maya refuses to give Elliot the watch without terms; she negotiates leverage
Scene Result: Elliot offers her operational cover in exchange for the watch and her cooperation; Maya agrees — conditionally
Outline Ref: turn

[OPENING STATE]
They are inside, back office. The desk lamp is the only light. The safe is on the back wall — closed. Maya stands between Elliot and the safe. Elliot has not been invited to sit. He doesn't try to.

[MAIN BODY]

MAYA
Tell me who you actually work for.

ELLIOT
Does it matter?

MAYA
It matters for what I'll agree to.

He pauses. A decision.

ELLIOT
An interagency task force. Not officially named. Technically decommissioned.

MAYA
Technically.

ELLIOT
We're very good at technically.

Maya picks up the burner card from the desk and holds it between two fingers.

MAYA
You printed this with a number that bypasses any registered exchange. That's not a task force. That's a black programme.

Elliot doesn't answer. That is an answer.

Maya sets the card down.

MAYA (CONT'D)
Here are my terms. You give me full immunity documentation — not verbal, signed — and you give me access to whatever you already know about the account. I hand you the watch. After that, I'm not a witness, I'm not an asset. I'm not in this.

ELLIOT
That's not realistic.

MAYA
Then leave without the watch and explain to your programme why nine hundred million dollars walked out in a velvet pouch.

A long silence. Elliot looks at the safe.

ELLIOT
I can give you the documentation. The access is conditional on what the credentials turn out to be.

MAYA
Full access. Non-conditional.

ELLIOT
(a beat)
Done.

Maya crosses to the safe. She dials the combination — three deliberate turns. The door swings open.

She reaches in.

Her hand stops.

[SCENE ENDING — cliffhanger]
She turns around slowly.

MAYA
Someone's already been in here.

The safe is empty. The velvet pouch is gone.
