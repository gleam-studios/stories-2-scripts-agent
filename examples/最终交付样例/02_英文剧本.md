## EPISODE 01

### SCENE 01.01
INT. HALE CHARITY GALA - NIGHT

Crystal light floods the ballroom. Donor portraits line every pillar. Guests orbit the room in careful formations.

Nora steps through the entrance alone. She doesn't scan for exits — she already knows them. She lifts a glass from the nearest tray and holds it without drinking.

Celia crosses the room with the practiced ease of a woman who owns it. She stops directly in Nora's path.

CELIA
You must be lost.

NORA
Not at all. I came exactly where I was invited.

Celia's smile tightens one degree — the only tell she allows.

### SCENE 01.02
INT. GALA SIDE CORRIDOR - NIGHT

Celia steers Nora out of the ballroom with social pressure alone — a hand, a redirect, a closed smile. The portrait of the original Nora Hale watches from the wall above them.

Celia turns. The corridor is narrow. This was the point.

CELIA
Whatever trick sent you through those doors ends tonight.

NORA
Then you should have locked them better.

Nora glances once at the portrait nameplate directly over Celia's shoulder. NORA HALE — HEIRESS. Then back at Celia. Nothing in her face.

ADRIAN (O.S.)
If you're done threatening the guests, I need Miss Stone for business.

Adrian steps out of the shadow at the corridor's end, wine glass loose, expression amused. Celia's jaw tightens. Nora doesn't move.
