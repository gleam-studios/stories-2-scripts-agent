## EPISODE 01

**Episode Core Event:** Maya buys a coded pocket watch, refuses to sell it back, and discovers the seller dead with a surveillance photo of herself.
**Episode Tone:** Quiet menace with rising surveillance paranoia.
**Key Reversal:** The danger is no longer abstract; Maya herself is already the target.
**Closing Hook:** Maya chooses the burner card over the police.
