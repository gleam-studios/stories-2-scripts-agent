## EPISODE 01

**Episode Core Event:** Maya buys a silver pocket watch from a panicked stranger; a mystery buyer immediately tries to purchase it back; that night the stranger is found dead with Maya's photograph in his pocket.
**Episode Tone:** Quiet menace — controlled surface, danger underneath.
**Key Reversal:** The dead man was carrying a photo of Maya taken through her own window — she is already being watched.
**Closing Hook:** Maya stands in the alley holding the photograph of herself, burner card in hand.
